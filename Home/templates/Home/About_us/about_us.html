Our Story

Born from three young minds in Nepal who believed cleaning could be smarter, safer, and more sustainable.

From Dorm Room to Your Home

It started with a simple question: Why do floor cleaners have to be 90% water? Three +2 students in Itahari – Rishab, Sudha, and Shreya – were frustrated with heavy liquid cleaners that cost more to ship than to make, and often contained harsh chemicals.

After months of research and testing with their academic knowledge combined with modern science, they created Nepal's first powder floor cleaner. Today, Saflora represents the spirit of young Nepal – innovative, sustainable, and deeply rooted in care for community.
