How to Use Saflora

Simple, effective cleaning in just 4 easy steps. Perfect for busy households and professional cleaning!

4 Simple Steps to Perfect Cleaning

1

Add Powder

Add 1 pack (50g) of Saflora powder to 1 liter of warm water.

Use warm water for better dissolution
2

Mix Well

Stir gently until powder completely dissolves and creates a light foam.

30 seconds of stirring is enough
3

Apply & Clean

Mop your floor as usual. The solution works on all floor types.

Works on marble, tiles, wood, and concrete
4

Enjoy Results

Let air dry or wipe with clean cloth. Enjoy fresh, clean floors!

No rinsing required - leaves no residue

Pro Tips from Our Users

For Daily Cleaning

Use slightly less powder for light, daily maintenance cleaning. Perfect for keeping your floors fresh between deep cleans.

For Deep Cleaning

Add an extra half tablespoon for stubborn stains and deep cleaning. Let it sit for a few minutes before mopping.

Storage Tip

Keep powder in cool, dry place. One packet lasts more than 6 months! Store away from moisture for best results.
